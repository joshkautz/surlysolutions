The Collection

This season's reserve

Twelve straws per vintage. When a bloodline is gone, it is gone. Each release is cellared at −196 °C and released to a waitlist of considered breeders.

All reservations are held for 14 days. Straws ship in vapour-phase dry shippers with a signed certificate of provenance.

Provenance

From paddock to paddock, honestly.

We believe genetics should be traceable to the blade of grass. Nothing anonymous. Nothing rushed. A process refined across four decades and three generations of the Vale family.

  1. 01

    Selection

    Our sommeliers taste — figuratively — hundreds of pedigrees. Fewer than two percent are invited to the barn.

  2. 02

    Collection

    Drawn by hand in a low-stress, classical-music-appointed suite. Each sire sets his own tempo.

  3. 03

    Cellaring

    Vitrified and held in single-origin cryo-vaults, each straw numbered and logged to its bloodline.

  4. 04

    Delivery

    Shipped in a vapour-phase vessel with a wax-sealed certificate. Cold-chain honest, always.

The Craft

Terroir you can trust.

A stallion is only ever as considered as the meadow beneath him. Our sires graze on limestone-rich pasture, rotate on a lunar schedule, and are never, ever hurried. The result is genetics with structure, length, and a quietly confident finish.

  • Limestone-forward, single-meadow pasture
  • Zero-rush, sommelier-supervised collection
  • Batch numbers traceable to the individual sire
  • Wax-sealed certificate of provenance with every order
